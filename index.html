About The Project

Ghaziabad's Most Premium High Street Destination

SKA Arcadia is a high-end Retail Street located in Wave City, NH-24 — India's first Pre-certified Platinum-rated Green Township. This futuristic commercial hub offers double-height shops, fine dining, anchor stores and lifestyle retail all under one prestigious address.

Developed by the SKA Group, one of Delhi-NCR's most trusted real estate developers, Arcadia is designed to become Ghaziabad's retail landmark — combining futuristic architecture with unmatched connectivity on NH-24.
